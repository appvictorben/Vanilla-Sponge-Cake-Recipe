Vanilla Sponge Cake

Enjoy this classic vanilla sponge cake recipe that is light, fluffy, and perfect for any occasion!

This light and fluffy vanilla sponge cake is perfect for birthdays, celebrations, or an afternoon tea treat. Simple ingredients and easy steps make it ideal for beginner bakers!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (120g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter (melted)
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour an 8-inch round cake pan.
  2. In a mixing bowl, beat eggs and sugar together until pale and fluffy.
  3. In another bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Gently fold dry ingredients into the egg mixture.
  5. Add melted butter, milk, and vanilla extract; mix until smooth.
  6. Pour batter into the prepared cake pan.
  7. Bake for 25–30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  8. Allow to cool for 10 minutes before removing from the pan.
  9. Serve plain or top with whipped cream and fresh fruit.

Tips

For extra fluffiness, make sure to beat the eggs and sugar long enough to trap plenty of air.
